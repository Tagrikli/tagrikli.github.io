Color-Based Product Recommendation App

13/09/2018

Developed an Android application that captures images and enables users to select specific regions to extract color data. Leveraged this to recommend matching nail products from an inventory.

The Challenge

The biggest challenge was understanding color spaces and color similarity. Different color spaces perceive and calculate color similarity differently, and some don't align with how humans actually perceive colors. This required deep research into color theory to ensure accurate product matching.

Solution

Built with pure Java in Android Studio, the application integrated computer vision techniques with real-world product mapping. Users could capture or select an image, tap on specific regions to extract colors, and receive recommendations for matching nail products from the inventory.

The project significantly enhanced my understanding of color spaces and their practical applications in consumer-facing products.
